How to Use HikeNow

📷 Camera — Overlay a photo as the map background. Tap anywhere to place a pin.

🛰️ GPS — Track your position in real time. Footprints are left automatically along your path.

🐛 Debug — Manually enter GPS coordinates for testing without a live signal.

🗺️ Map Mode — Enables pin placement anywhere, even without GPS or a photo.

📍 Pins — Click a placed pin to show its toolbar. Use arrow keys to nudge or N/S/E/W to adjust by 1 meter. Copy coordinates or delete the pin.

👣 Clear Trail — Erase all footprints while keeping your reference pins.

💡 Tip — Place at least two reference pins on features you recognize (trail junctions, landmarks) to enable GPS-to-pixel mapping and automatic footprint tracking.
