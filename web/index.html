10 Hiking Essentials

  1. Navigation — paper map & compass
  2. Headlamp — with extra batteries
  3. Sun protection — sunscreen, sunglasses, sun-protective clothing
  4. First aid kit — including moleskin/blister care
  5. Knife / multi-tool
  6. Fire starter — matches, lighter, tinder, candle
  7. Emergency shelter — bivy sack or emergency blanket
  8. Extra food — calorie-dense snacks beyond what you expect
  9. Extra water — more than you think, plus purification
  10. Extra clothing — insulating layers and rain gear (avoid cotton)

How to Use HikeNow

Camera — Overlay a photo as the map background. Tap anywhere to place a pin.

Map Mode — Enables pin placement anywhere, even without GPS or a photo.

GPS — Track your position in real time. Footprints are left automatically along your path.

Rotate — Rotate the map image 90° counter-clockwise.

Elevation — Show/hide the elevation profile of your trail.

Replay — Animate your trail from start to finish.

Path Style — Cycle between circle footprints, a line, or both.

Clear Trail — Erase all footprints while keeping your reference pins.

Distance — Click to toggle between metric (m/km) and imperial (ft/mi).

Pins — Click a placed pin to show its toolbar. Use arrow keys to nudge or N/S/E/W to adjust by 1 meter. Copy coordinates or delete the pin.

10 Essentials — A handy checklist of hiking essentials.

Reload — Check for app updates and reload if a newer version is available.

Debug — Manually enter GPS coordinates for testing without a live signal.

💡 Tip — Place at least two reference pins on features you recognize (trail junctions, landmarks) to enable GPS-to-pixel mapping and automatic footprint tracking.
